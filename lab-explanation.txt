This solution keeps the starter idea and adds the required design patterns.

1) Singleton
Used in LibraryService so there is only one service object.

2) Factory Method
Used in BookFactory to create different book types.

3) Decorator
Used in PremiumBook to add 10 extra borrow days without changing Book.

4) Chain of Responsibility
Used in Librarian, Manager, and Director to approve requests depending on days.

5) Facade
Used in LibraryFacade to simplify borrowing operations.

6) Adapter
Used in BookAdapter to convert external book data to our book format.

7) Observer
Used in NotifiableBook to notify users when a book becomes available.
